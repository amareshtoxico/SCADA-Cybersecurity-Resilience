## üéØ Problem Statement

Traditional SCADA systems rely on **legacy protocols (Modbus, DNP3)** that lack encryption and authentication. Combined with weak access control and limited intrusion detection, these systems are highly vulnerable to cyberattacks such as unauthorized access, DoS attacks, and manipulation of industrial processes.
